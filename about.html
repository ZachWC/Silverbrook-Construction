About Silverbrook Construction

Welcome to Silverbrook Construction, your trusted partner for home building and remodeling in Boise, Idaho. We are dedicated to turning your dream home into a reality with our expertise in new builds, remodeling, flooring, painting, and more.

Our mission is to provide exceptional craftsmanship and personalized service to every client. We believe in building strong relationships based on trust, integrity, and open communication. Our team of experienced professionals is committed to delivering high-quality results that exceed your expectations.

At Silverbrook Construction, we understand that your home is more than just a place to live; it's a reflection of your lifestyle and personality. That's why we work closely with you to understand your vision and bring it to life. Whether you're looking to build a new home, remodel your existing space, or enhance your interiors with new flooring and paint, we have the skills and expertise to make it happen.

We take pride in our attention to detail and commitment to quality. From the initial design phase to the final touches, we ensure that every aspect of your project is handled with care and precision. Our goal is to create spaces that are not only beautiful but also functional and tailored to your specific needs.

Thank you for considering Silverbrook Construction for your home building and remodeling needs. We look forward to working with you and creating the home of your dreams.
